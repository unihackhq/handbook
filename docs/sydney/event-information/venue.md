# Venue

This year's competition will be held at Stone and Chalk, which is located
at:

Level 2, AMP Tower <br>
50 Bridge Street <br>
Sydney NSW 2000

## How do I get there?

As the venue is near Circular Quay Station, the best way of getting to 271 Collins Street is by train. If taking a train, simply walk down Young Street and turn left onto Bridge Street.
